About Me

A Bucknell Senior CS Major

Danny Toback

I am a senior at Bucknell University actively seeking to apply my skills to a full-time position in the near future. My first true love in Computer Science was Software Development in Java, though this whole Web-Development thing is pretty fun. I took full advantage of my liberal arts education by earning minors in both Economics and Spanish. An obligatory fun fact about me is that I was the Drum Captain of my State Champion marching band. While born and raised in sunny Long Island, I hope to make my next big step by moving to The Big City (Manhattan).

Bucknell University

My decision to attend Bucknell University was, without a doubt, one of the best decisions I’ve ever made. Bucknell has given me the opportunity to not only perfect my skills in Computer Science, but take classes in a variety of subjects, and become the best version of myself that I can be.

Interests

My interests range from Web Design to Software Design, and everything in-between. I look forward to tackling whatever challenges I’m tasked with, from creating an Python program to help study for finals, to helping fix a computer at my on campus job at the Tech Desk.

Hobbies

In my spare time I enjoy watching the New York Yankees, Giants, Knicks, and Islanders play in their respective sports. As the son of a former book store owner, I do my best to keep up with best sellers, but haven’t had much time whilst in college.
